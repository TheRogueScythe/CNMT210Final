Painkiller

  • Find 4 Morphine injectors in raid
  • Hand over 4 Morphine injectors to Therapist

The best places to look for these are the medical sites on Customs, Woods, Or Reserve. This task can be completed by getting the items in succesful PMC or Scav raids.
