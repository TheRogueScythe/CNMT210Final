Sanitary Standards - Part 1

  • Find 1 Gas analyzer in raid
  • (Optional) Gain access to the locked pumping station on Factory
  • Hand over the 1 Gas analyzer to Therapist

These can spawn in most technical places, such as mechanic boxes or tech rooms. This is a task the can be completed with succesful PMC or Scav raids
