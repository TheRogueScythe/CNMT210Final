Delivery from the Past

  • Obtain the secure folder in the Tarcone Director's office at the Customs terminal warehouse
  • Stash the package in the Factory break room (2nd floor near Gate 3)
  • Survive and extract from Factory

This quest can be tricky. First you need the Directors key which can be found in jacket pockets. Next, get the package, survive the extract and load into factory. While in factory, make it to the metal room (up the metal stairs near gate 3) and plant the package. After the plant, you no longer need to worry about dying. The survive part unlocks after the plant, and can be completed in any PMC factory raid after that subtask is unlocked.
