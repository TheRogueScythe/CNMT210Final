Checking

  • Obtain the Bronze pocket watch on Customs
  • (Optional) Obtain the key to the fuel tanker truck
  • Hand over the Bronze pocket watch to Prapor

For this quest, you are first going to need the Machinery key, found in room 205 of 3 story dorms. Next, find the orange feul tanker near skeleton (the smallest of the 3 concrete structures), near the T in the main road. Pickup the pocket watch, and extrtact.
