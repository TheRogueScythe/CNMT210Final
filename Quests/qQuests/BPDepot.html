BP Depot

  • Mark the first fuel tank with an MS2000 Marker on Customs
  • Mark the second fuel tank with an MS2000 Marker on Customs
  • Mark the third fuel tank with an MS2000 Marker on Customs
  • Mark the fourth fuel tank with an MS2000 Marker on Customs
  • Survive and extract from the location

There are 2 big tips for this quest. The first, These feul tankers are very large and very orange. All are very visible and you should not have much of an issue finding them. The second, you do not have to survive the same raid that you plant the mark. As long as the mark is placed, that part is completed. After all 4 marks are placed, you must survive ANY customs PMC raid.
