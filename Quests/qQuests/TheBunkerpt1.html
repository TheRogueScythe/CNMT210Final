The Bunker - Part 1

  • Find the underground bunker
  • Locate the control room in the underground bunker
  • Survive and extract from the location

The bunker is often a highly contested area, as it is the main way to extract on this map. It is often good to wait until later in the raid to enter the bunker, and then move quickly through the objectives and to the D2 extract. Watch out for extract campers.
