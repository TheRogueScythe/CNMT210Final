Debut

  • Eliminate 5 Scavs all over the Tarkov territory
  • Obtain and hand over 2 MP-133 12ga shotguns

Since this quest is not map specific, you are free to chose the location you wish to eliminate scavs. While doing so, check to see if the scav is carrying a MP-133 shotgun, and pick up one if it is marked "found in raid". Another way to get this shotgun is to Unlock\ Jaeger, who sells the weapon.
